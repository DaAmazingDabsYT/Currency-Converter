ABOUT CODEYOUNG

Codeyoung is the platform where your kids can let their imagination run wild. Students are taught to be creative, innovative, and use coding in the most unusual way possible.

Founded by Shailendra Dhakad and Rupika Taneja, Codeyoung is Live Interactive coding platform for kids. Globally active with 20,000+ students from 10+ countries. Our courses are well distributed and tailored for different age groups.
